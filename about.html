About

Bridge Professional Development Centre, training provider in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia.

  • How we began

    We set out to offer the best training program in soft skills and languages for individuals, adults and children, groups and organisations back in 2015. From a small beginning we have grown in size and capability to offer an ever widening prospectus that is meeting the needs of all those who choose us. We now occupy a wonderful centrally located premises in which is both a location for classes and a hub for learning.

  • A wide range of learning opportunities

    Soft Skills
    We offer a range of soft skill courses which are very practical for the 21st century world of work. Some of our popular soft skill courses include: Leadership, Communication, Customer Service and Team Working skills.
    Language Courses
    Our language courses focus on two areas. We offer preparation course for the IELTS Life Skills and IELTS Academic Tests. We also offer Business English courses, face to face or virtually.
    Intercultural Courses
    For expatriates in Addis Ababa, we have excellent courses to foster and enable cultural competence and ease. Our offer includes an Intercultural Course, Introduction to Ethiopian Culture and Amharic for Expatriates.
    For Teachers and Educators
    Our staff are highly experienced teacher trainers and school leaders. We have a number of courses available which can be tailored to meet the needs of people in your school.

  • We Meet Your Needs

    People have different schedules and different amounts of time available, so our development centre offers different class options – schedule, size, location and duration - that will give you the fullest flexibility, where you need it.

  • Things You Should Know About Us!

    We,

    • provide the best training in soft skills and languages for individuals, groups and organisations in Addis Ababa

    • are located in a superb easily accessible area, and provide professional learning facilities and resources.

    • work with only the best professionals dedicated to provide a world class learning and development interventions.

    • have a welcoming, warm atmosphere.
