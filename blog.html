Learning with your Team - the Awayday

One of the most overlooked tools in the learning and development toolbox used by managers is the Away-day. That’s a pity because it has many benefits. Not the “let’s-build-a-boat/bridge-with-straws-and-sticky-tape-and-cross-the-river” kind. More like this “a day where colleagues come together, away from work, to explore, resolve and plan”.
