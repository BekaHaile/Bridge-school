About Us

Bridge School of Languages is your first choice for language learning and communication skills in Addis Ababa.
We specialise in affordable and effective face-to-face and on-line training in languages, management, communication, administration, personal effectiveness and writing skills delivered by a team of specialist and experts. We work with individuals (self or company sponsored) and organisations of all sizes across the public, NGO and commercial sectors.

About Our Language Courses

Bridge offers a wide range of local and foreign language courses and learning options delivered with professionalism, dedication to quality and … panache. You’ll see the difference:

  • Your course can be delivered as a standard programme or we can build an option that is customised to you, regardless of level – beginner to advanced

Four learning options for you

1)PERSONAL : this is a one-to-one option where you work with a dedicated trainer or teacher. Choose this if you need flexibility in your schedule, your learning path and learning speed.
2)GROUP : you may prefer a more social experience joining a group of between 6 and 20 students, of similar level and needs to you. The group interaction adds colour and texture and lots of opportunities for practice and feedback. This group option is scheduled Monday, Wednesday and Friday with 90-minute classes each day.
3)SMALL GROUPS: groups of friends, families and work colleagues choose this option where they value small group size – between 3 and 5 students – and familiarity. These small groups often share the same goals the teacher can respond with focused and relevant activities.
4)ONLINE LEARNING: many choose this learning option when they are unable to join a face-to-face class for personal or business reasons. You can do this as a single learner or join others in a similar situation. Going online has all the same ingredients as face-to-face learning … but without the travel!

About

Bridge School of Languages, a language school in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia.

  • How we began

    We set out to offer the best language learning programs for individuals, adults and children, groups and organisations back in 2015. From a small beginning we have grown in size and capability to offer an ever widening prospectus that is meeting the needs of all those who choose us. We now occupy a wonderful centrally located premises in which is both a location for classes and a hub for learning.

  • A wide range of learning opportunities

    Bridge offers programs and courses in English, Amharic, French and a wide range of other local and foreign languages such as Academic English, Business English, English for Email and other specialised areas. For those focused on qualifications and attainment we also provide different exam preparation programs for IELTS, SAT, TOEFL and other national and international English exams.

  • We Meet Your Needs

    People have different schedules and different amounts of time available, so our school offers different class options – schedule, size, location and duration - that will give you the fullest flexibility, where you need it.

  • Things You Should Know About Us!

    We,

Abayneh Haile

Director of Studies

I am the owner and director of Bridge School of Languages. I am passionate about education. Here are some of the milestones of my career, in Ethiopia and abroad. I have an MA from Leeds University, UK in Teacher Education. I am also UK CELTA qualified to teach English to adults, a widely recognised and respected base qualification. I am very active in my own personal professional development and I am engaged with supporting others in the profession wherever I can.
