Welcome To Bridge Professional Development Centre


Train, Coach, and Empower!
Tell Me More

Established in 2015, Bridge Professional Development Centre is a premier training and coaching provider based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia.
We specialize in delivering affordable and effective face-to-face and online training in soft skills. We offer one-on-one and team coaching in key areas like Life and Leadership Coaching, Executive Coaching, and Career Coaching — all facilitated by experienced, ICF-certified coaches. We also facilitate retreat programs for organizations for team building purposes. We also teach English for Specific Purpose. We are able to design a tailored made English language programs for a specific role. Catering to individuals, groups, and organizations across the public, NGO, and commercial sectors, we offers programs that are stretching, motivating, and tailored to individuals. As a social responsibility, we run a Reading Champions program designed to promote reading for pleasure by helping children develop strong and lasting reading habits. Our mission is to train, coach, and empower clients to reach their full potential, both professionally and personally.

You’ll spend more time ‘on the business’ rather than ‘in the business’. Away together, learning together and achieving together.

As your facilitator, we will develop a programme to suit the unique dynamics of your people and the outcomes you want to achieve. Whether you need to create your strategic plan, decide on a new product, process or campaign, get to know your team better or simply take stock of the past financial year and look to the year ahead, we can create the day you need.

For your Awayday we have tools to examine how your team is doing, team building activities, indoor games, energizers, sports, evening games, campfire activities and more.

We have facilitated team building sessions for various organisations including: Mastercard Foundation, WHO, World Vision, Kefeta, CST Ethiopia and Capital Hotel & Spa.

Amharic for expats

From greetings to conversations

The training focuses on developing basic communication skills for everyday interactions, alongside reading and writing which includes vocabulary and grammar lessons. Participants learn common phrases and thematic vocabulary related to various aspects of life, while also gaining essential grammar knowledge about sentence structure, tenses, and verbs.

Speaking and listening practice is emphasized through conversational exercises with native speakers and listening activities to enhance comprehension. Cultural immersion is an integral part of the program, providing insights into Ethiopian customs, music, literature, and social etiquette.

Intercultural skills training

being your best self

Reflecting on what “culture” is, and how it influences our preferences and behaviours in intercultural situations, especially in the workplace, is the best starting point for personal success.

You will understand new and familiar cultural situations and consider how others may react to our behaviours and style. This will enable you to respond positively and empathetically, building trust, familiarity and collegiality.

This 1-day course will help you ‘be you at your best’ wherever the context, in business conversations and meetings, interacting with colleagues, customers and clients.

Reading Champions

Raising a generation of children who enjoy children!

Many children in Ethiopia associate reading with tests and exams. The only time children read is when they are learning something and are expected to do it as part of their studies. The concept of reading for pleasure doesn’t exist in many parts of Ethiopia. The Reading Champions program aims to contribute towards reading for pleasure, this is the overarching goal of the programme. We would like to help children cultivate reading habits. We want them to read for the sake of reading. Reading in its own is a reward.

The following are some of the activities we do in our programme.

  • invite children to read their favourite stories on our monthly get together - come-and-read-your-story! Encourage children who come to our monthly meetings to donate books to those who can’t afford to buy
  • train schools on reading for pleasure especially in relation to using their library well.
  • run reading challenge programmes and other competitions
  • organise panel discussions and festivals in the area of literacy
  • do surveys and research in the area of literacy
  • build relationships with other local and international organisations which work in the area of literacy

Teacher training

Empowering educators

This practical and interactive short training equips teachers with the tools and strategies to integrate 21st-century skills into their classrooms. Participants will explore key competencies, including:

  • Critical thinking and problem solving
  • Creativity and imagination
  • Citizenship
  • Communication and collaboration
  • Digital literacy 
  • Leadership and personal development.
Through hands-on activities and real-world examples, educators will learn how to cultivate these skills in students, preparing them for success in an ever-evolving world. This is ideal for teachers at all levels who seek to enhance their teaching with future-ready approaches.

School leadership training

Shaping the future of education

A one point increase on the school management practices of school leaders is associated with a ten percent increase in student performance. (Research by Stanford University)

Our School Leaders Training program is designed to empower educational leaders with the skills, knowledge, and strategies necessary to drive positive change within their schools. This comprehensive program focuses on fostering effective leadership practices, enhancing communication skills, and promoting a collaborative school culture. Participants will engage in interactive workshops, case studies, and peer discussions that address real-world challenges faced by school leaders today.

Lead, Develop, Care

Leadership framework

In today’s fast-moving and complex world, effective leadership requires more than just managing tasks—it demands clarity, compassion, and the ability to develop others. The *Lead, Develop, Care* coaching program is designed to help leaders focus on what truly matters: building momentum, nurturing talent, and creating a culture of trust and care. Through a blend of practical strategies and personalized coaching, this program equips you to lead with intention, support growth in others, and foster a thriving, resilient team environment. Whether you're an emerging leader or a seasoned executive, this program will elevate your leadership journey and strengthen your impact.
