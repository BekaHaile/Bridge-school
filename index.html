About Our Language Courses

Bridge School of Languages offers different language courses that are interestingly delivered by professional teachers.

  • Most are native speakers of the language targeted by customers.
  • We provide both local and foreign languages to our students efficiently and effectively.
  • Our curriculum focuses on reading, writing, vocabulary building, listening and speaking skills.
  • We have an interactive teaching method that allows students to develop their communicative skill in a fair amount of time.
  • Bridge has four different class structures along with different time and price packages that can easily allow students to choose the type of class they prefer and the time schedule that suits them with a price package they can afford.

Bridge’s four different Class Structures.

1)Personal Class: a one-to-one class structure where an individual student and a teacher communicate directly and join in the learning and teaching process. Personal classes are highly recommended for people who’d like to customize their schedules. It’s also convenient for people who prefer direct communication with their teacher and seek for special attention. People with a tight schedule and a busy life style usually find this class very suitable.
2)Group Class: a class of minimum six and maximum twenty students. It is a highly recommended form of language class since it's interactive and helps students develop their communicative skill in an adequate amount of time.

Group classes are diverse since different people from different places attend them and this creates a nice , vibrant atmosphere where people can actively learn.It’s provided three times a week (Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays) for one and a half hours a day .
3)Small Groups: gatherings of minimum three and maximum five students and are preferable for people who enjoy learning in a less crowded atmosphere and for groups of friends, families or colleagues who’d like to learn a new language together.
4)Online Classes: - a class held online using virtual learning methods. It allows students to learn from where they are without having to come to school. It’s a very convenient method nowadays due to people’s tight schedule and recently due to the pandemic.

Other Special Programs

Bridge offers exam preparation programs for IELTS, SAT, TOEFL and other national and international language exams. We have teachers who are specifically qualified to train students and prepare them for the exams in the desired amount of time. Our school has resources that students can use to prepare themselves.

Junior Programs

Junior programs are meant for students in the age range of (5-15). This program helps kids attend language classes and activities such as “The Reading Challenge “and other different interesting activities for children. It’s held during the summer and it helps children to learn different languages and explore their hobbies and talents through different writing, reading, acting, singing and drawing sessions along the way.

Bridge offers

Five level classes to provide a better learning / teaching atmosphere and a class that perfectly suits every student. After taking a placement test, students’ level can easily be determined and we can classify them as a beginner, elementary, pre intermediate, intermediate or as an advanced student in the language they’ve chosen to learn.

About

Bridge School of Languages, a language school in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia.

  • Our Humble Beginning

    Since 2015 our school has been giving different language courses with a curriculum that has helped students communicate effectively and grow passion for languages. Throughout the years, Bridge has been offering a variety of language courses and special programs designed for both children and adults.

  • Everything we provide

    Bridge offers programs and courses in English, Amharic, French and many other local and foreign languages. The school gives a variety of English courses in Academic English, Business English, English for Email and more. There are also different exam preparation programs for IELTS, SAT, TOEFL and other national and international English exams.

  • We Try To Meet Your Needs

    People have different schedules and different amounts of time available, so our school offers different forms of classes that enable customers to adjust and customize their schedules and effectively attend the courses.
